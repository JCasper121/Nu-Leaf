About Us

These days property costs are rising like the sea levels. Many people aren't in the position to buy a house, and renting is even more expensive. Coupled with the cost of utilities and the environmental footprint of traditional homes, many people today are re-thinking housing.

Tiny Homes, as the name implies, are smaller, but make resourceful use of the space they have. They're cheaper to heat and cool. They can be mobile. They can be built to use natural phenomena, like sunlight and rain to your advantage. All this makes for a cheaper, cleaner, and more sustainable lifestyle. That's why many people are turning to tiny homes!
